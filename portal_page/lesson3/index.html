Site Purpose:

Momentum is a local organization of businesses and companies in Selborne, East London with the intention to develop and further the interests of local companies and businesses in South Africa. Many businesses are international operating companies with offices in South Africa. The purpose of the site is to advertise our organization what it does. We safeguard business interests and share business experiences and business interests, contact with governments, civil society, local media and the press and organzing trade shows and events. This site is designed to show what we have to offer and how you can be apart of that. It is vital that we help businesses prosper and grow.
