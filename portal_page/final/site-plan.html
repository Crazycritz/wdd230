Overview

Purpose

The mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. Promote healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience. Increase the business' online presence.

Audience

The local population and tourists in the coastal areas who enjoy our products, have access to internet and wish to order online.

Personas

James is a 28 year old working in the field of software development. He has to work remotely(mostly from home) but still desires a morning fruit drink to get his day going. He orders from the Bountiful Foods online website for delivery to his current work space.

Susan is a 34 year old mom who has to be up early to take the care of her children and get them to school on time. Due to this she does not have much time to do shopping. She orders from the Bountiful Foods website in bulk and has fresh fruits delivered to her door step, this saves her a lot of time and allows her to easily carry on with her day to day requirements.

Visitor's purpose in visiting

-They wish to order our products online and have them delivered

-Easy to access and order (only need internet)

-They can easily find out the price of our products

-All our contact information is displayed so they can reach out to us

-The site has good UX design
