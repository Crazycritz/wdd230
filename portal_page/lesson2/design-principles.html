Design Principles - Joshua Mostert

Alignment

blizzard.com

Blizzard uses the principle of alignment very well in their website. Each element on the page is visually connected to each other and is not randomly place. This connection with the elements allows the site to easily flow and looks organized.

Hick's Law

amazon.com

Amazon uses the principle of Hick's Law perfectly on their site. Hick's Law states that the time and the effort it takes to make a decision, increases with the number of options. Amazon grealy reduces that time buy sorting and catergorizing their website majorly.

White Space and Clean Design

istore.co.za

I have noticed that Apple loves to use white space. They use the principle of white space very well in their website. Elements are perfectly spaced to highlight each of them respectively. This helps their website look structured, organized and improves readability.
